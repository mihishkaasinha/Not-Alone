What's Next


Here are the functionalities we plan to include in the next versions:


  • Login: Implement login functionality for User and Orphanage

  • Online Payment options: ( eg: Net Banking, Google Pay, PayTM) integration

  • Orphanage Verification: This is one of the Most critical functionality. We will build a manual verification and approval process before allowing any orphanage to be shown in our solution, after the Orphanage has submitted the registration request. We have to ensure that we only authentic and verified Orphanages on our solution page

  • Company Registration: we can have companies to register and make donations. We can ask them to pay us some amount for using our solution and give advertisements

  • Job Posting: Company can post jobs that they think can be done by Orphanage members especially the ones with a disability

  • Items published by User: We have lots of unused/old items that we would love to give to Orphanage. But most of the times we throw it away. We will provide the user with the option to upload such items details and Orphanage can book those items

  • Education Request: Orphanage can request education on certain topics and any User can conduct the sessions free of cost

  • Systematic Help Plan (SHP): My father always talks about investing through SIP. He gave me the idea that we can allow User to set monthly/yearly SHP of any amount to the orphanage of their choice
